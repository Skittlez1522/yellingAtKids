About Us

We are an airline company that flies to 13 cities in 7 different states in the central region of the US. These states include Colorado, Utah, Washington, Idaho, Montana, South Dakota, Wyoming, and Nebraska.

We have a fleet of 15 Airbus A320neos. These planes are comfortable single aisle aircraft that are kept in pristine condition by our top notch mechanical staff.

We have two different major destinations: Denver and Salt Lake City. These cities have a lot to offer.

Denver and the Denver Metropolitan Area is a beautiful place. The Rocky Mountains sit to the west of Denver. To the east are the Great Plains. South of Denver are cities which have top tourist destinations of their own. To the north of Denver is CSU and Wyoming.

The Salt Lake City region boasts beautiful views from every direction you face. Not only are there endless amounts of trails to hike on and bike, but there is also the Great Salt Lake, which is the largest lake west of the Mississippi River and the largest salt lake in the US.

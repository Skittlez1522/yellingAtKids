What does Einstein's theory explain?

Einstein's theory explains gravity as a distortion of the fabric of space caused by the presence of matter or energy.

This theory explains how gravity occurs while Newton explained that gravity does exist universally.

Einstein developed this theory in the early 1900's as part of his theory of relativity which is one of the most widely known theories in the world
